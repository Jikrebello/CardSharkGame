How to Play

Pick one of two cards each turn. One is safe loot, one is the shark.

Goal

Build a run by collecting unique loot (1–7), then Bank before you bust. Banked points add to your total score.

Busting

  • Hit the shark without a shield → lose a life. If you run out of all 3 lives, the run ends.
  • Draw a duplicate number in the same run (without shield) → lose your streak, but not your lives.

Jokers

  • Shield: blocks one shark or duplicate bust.
  • Scrambler: rerolls a duplicate number (limited charges).
  • Jammer: temporarily helps reduces the shark from appearing.

Turn Flow

  1. Pick a card
  2. Resolve loot / shark
  3. Choose to continue or Bank
  4. Survive to maximize score

Trophies

Your final score determines your trophy tier: Bronze, Silver, or Gold.
